Shooting Stars

In this painting, Monet captures the mirrored reflections of his garden through his distinctive brushstrokes and vibrant colors. Gentle strokes of emerald, azure, and amethyst caress the serene pond, creating a sense of tranquility. The sheer size of the canvas ensures the viewer can fully experience the beauty of the idyllic setting.
